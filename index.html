AMARITA MEXICAN DICTIONARY

Hocicon

Literal: Big snout.
Meaning

Loudmouth, trash talker, or someone who talks a massive amount of shit but cannot back it up.

Stream Context
When someone is talking shit in proximity chat and you kill them. "Tan hocicon y no la armas, a chingar a su madre pa la lobby."

Mamón

Literal: Sucker.
Meaning

Arrogant, insufferable, a jerk, or someone who is trying way too hard.

Stream Context
Perfect for when the lobby is ridiculously sweaty. If a mf is slide canceling 40 times, YY'n like crazy, and doing crazy movement just to get a basic kill, they are being a mamón. "Ay, qué mamón eres."

Puñetas

Literal: Wanks.
Meaning

A complete idiot, a wanker, or someone who is absolutely useless. Describes someone making phenomenally stupid decisions.

Stream Context
You see/watch someone do something really stupid and dumb. "Estas bien puñetas wey."

Me la pelas

Literal: You peel it for me (a banana).
Meaning

"You can't touch me," "You are nothing compared to me," or "I am better than you."

Stream Context
When a tryhard mf tries to ego chall u but you completely outgun him and leave him crawling on the floor. "Me la pelaste wey."

Chamaco Miado

Literal: Piss covered kid.
Meaning

A deeply disrespectful and dismissive way to call someone a brat, a "squeaker," or an immature child who hasn't earned the right to talk trash.

Stream Context
When a little kid or a grown ass man with childish behavior starts screaming every profanity they know. "Ya cállese, pinche chamaco miado."

Hambreado

Literal: Starving or famished.
Meaning

Extremely thirsty for a kill/playing utterly desperately.

Stream Context
Use this when an enemy player chases you across the entire map, ignoring other teams and the gas, just to get one single kill. "No seas tan hambreado wey."

Llorón

Literal: Crier.
Meaning

Crybaby or complainer.

Stream Context
When you kill someone and immediately hear them screaming in death comms about aim assist, lag, or accusing her of hacking because their ego is bruised. "No seas llorón."

Espantaviejas

Literal: Woman scarer.
Meaning

A guy whose behavior/personality repels women. Tryhards, incels, and random dudes who talk unprovoked trash the second they hear a girl in game chat.

Stream Context
If a random guy hears your voice in proximity chat and instantly starts making kitchen jokes or acting weird. "Mi compa el espantaviejas."

Culón

Literal: Someone with a big ass.
Meaning

A massive coward or a pussy. Someone who talks a big game but completely backs down when the actual fight starts.

Stream Context
Lets say an enemy shoots you from the back, you turn around and break him, then he starts running. "Pa que corres, no seas culon."

Agüitado / Agüitarse

Literal: To get watery.
Meaning

Feeling down, completely defeated, bummed out, or losing your motivation after something bad happens.

Stream Context
When you lose a 1v1 you know you should have won, or a teammate immediately gets fully killed off the drop and chat can tell the vibes are down. "Ando bien agüitada." OR "No te agüites, wey."

Curada

Literal: Cured.
Meaning

Cool, funny, or awesome.

Stream Context
Lets say you watch a crazy clip or someone in chat says something genuinely funny. "Qué curada está ese clip."

Arre

Literal: Giddy-up.
Meaning

Say less, bet, I'm down.

Stream Context
Someone in chat ask you if you want a chall or to run a specific loadout. "Arre arre."
